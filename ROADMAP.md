# Roadmap

## v0.1 public alpha

- Signed and notarized Apple-Silicon release.
- Built-in, USB, and Bluetooth hardware validation.
- Reconnect, sleep/wake, and source-relaunch resilience.
- GUI/CLI state coherence and release health reporting.

## v0.2 beta

- Universal Apple Silicon + Intel release.
- AudioConverter output-format adapter for non-Float32 devices.
- Device compatibility reports and richer diagnostics.
- Homebrew cask distribution.

## Explicitly deferred

- Automatic acoustic calibration.
- Google Cast transmitter support.
- Guaranteed Roku/AirPlay receiver support.
- Multi-app mixing, recording, EQ, and remote streaming.
