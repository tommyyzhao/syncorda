# Syncorda Code of Conduct

We want Syncorda to be a respectful, practical, and welcoming project. Treat contributors, users, and maintainers with consideration.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive and assume good intent.
- Discuss ideas and technical trade-offs without personal attacks.
- Respect privacy: never request or publish audio recordings, credentials, private account data, or identifying device details unless the reporter has chosen to share them.
- Accept moderation decisions made to keep the project safe and productive.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized language, doxxing, deliberate disruption, and abusive conduct are not welcome.

## Reporting and enforcement

For a private report, use the repository's private vulnerability-reporting channel and clearly label the report as a conduct concern. Maintainers will acknowledge and assess reports as promptly as practical, protect reporter confidentiality where possible, and may warn, remove content, restrict participation, or ban participants.

This code applies in all Syncorda project spaces.
